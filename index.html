I woke up with dry eyes and my stomach hurting like hell. Where was I and where were my shoes? I Turned my Head left. Nothing. I turned my head right.

I looked around and figured I was in a tiny cell with no windows. In front of me was a door. It had big red letter forming the word EXIT. “Perfect”, I thought and walked through. On the other side I was greeted by a horde of zombies. Dang. I slowly turned around and opened my wallet. Luckily my brother gave me this small foldable credit-card-knife last year. So I flicked it open, slashed the first zombie, kicked the next and jumped over a couple more, running to the end of the corridor.
